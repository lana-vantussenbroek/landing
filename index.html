What is SkiHUB?

SkiHUB is the first ski social media, where you will be able to share pictures, videos, perfs and comments about ski. You will be able to follow your favorite skiers, and reach out to them via the comment, and chat sections. You can also interact with your friends and your ski mates, and keep track of their competition perfs, and their speed records.
